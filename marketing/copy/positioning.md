# Positioning & messaging

## One-liner
**Describe it. Ship a Shopify app.**

## Elevator pitch
ApexBuild is the AI app builder made for Shopify. Describe a merchant tool in
plain English and get an installable, Built-for-Shopify app on Shopify's official
React Router template — App Bridge, Polaris, Admin GraphQL, webhooks, and billing
already wired in — grounded in your real store's catalog and scopes. What used to
take a developer (or a $15k agency) takes a sentence.

## Who it's for
1. **Merchants** who need a custom tool their theme/app stack doesn't offer.
2. **Agencies & Shopify partners** who build client apps and want to 10× their throughput.
3. **Developers** who want a correct, compliant starting point instead of a blank repo.

## The problem
Generic AI app builders (Lovable, Bolt, v0) produce React that doesn't
authenticate with Shopify, doesn't use Polaris, and won't pass App Store review.
Building a real Shopify app means OAuth, session storage, webhooks, the Admin
GraphQL API, protected-data compliance, and Built-for-Shopify standards — hours
of undifferentiated plumbing before you write a single feature.

## Why ApexBuild wins
- **Installable output, not a demo.** Runs with `shopify app dev`; authenticates on every route.
- **Grounded in your real store.** Connect your shop; generation uses your actual products, collections, and scopes.
- **Correct by construction.** Admin GraphQL operations validated against the live API; a deterministic Built-for-Shopify compliance gate; mandatory privacy webhooks included.
- **Ready to submit.** Every project ships a pre-filled App Store submission kit.
- **You own 100% of the code.**
