## Description (≤ 260 chars)
ApexBuild turns a sentence into an installable, Built-for-Shopify app — App
Bridge, Polaris, Admin GraphQL, webhooks & billing wired in, grounded in your
real store. Skip the plumbing, ship the feature. You own 100% of the code.

## First comment (maker's note)
Hey Product Hunt 👋

We kept watching merchants and Shopify partners burn days on the *same* plumbing
for every app — OAuth, session storage, webhooks, Polaris, the Admin GraphQL API,
protected-data compliance — before writing a single feature. And generic AI
builders couldn't help: they output React that doesn't even authenticate with
Shopify.

So we built **ApexBuild**: describe a merchant tool in plain English and get an
**installable** app on Shopify's official React Router template. It's:

- 🛍️ **Grounded in your real store** — connect your shop and it generates against your actual catalog, collections, and scopes.
- ✅ **Built for Shopify by default** — a deterministic compliance gate + mandatory privacy webhooks on every build.
- ⚡ **Fast** — a sentence to a runnable app (`shopify app dev`), with a pre-filled App Store submission kit.
- 🔓 **Yours** — export the whole project; no lock-in.

Free to start (3 builds/mo). We'd love your hardest "I wish a Shopify app did X"
ideas in the comments — we'll generate them live. 🚀

## Maker reply snippets
- **"How is this different from Lovable/Bolt?"** Those are horizontal — great for generic web apps, but they don't produce a *Shopify* app (no App Bridge/Polaris/Admin API auth, won't pass review). ApexBuild is vertical: installable, compliant, grounded in your store.
- **"Do I own the code?"** 100%. Download the full project anytime; run it with the Shopify CLI.
- **"Does it pass App Store review?"** We bake in the Built-for-Shopify checks and mandatory privacy webhooks, and generate a submission kit — you still review and submit, but you start at the finish line.
