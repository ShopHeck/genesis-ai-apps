## Detailed description

**Build the Shopify tool you wish existed — without hiring a developer.**

ApexBuild turns plain-English ideas into installable, Built-for-Shopify apps.
Describe what you need ("flag products under a reorder point and email me a daily
digest") and ApexBuild generates a complete app on Shopify's official template —
App Bridge, Polaris UI, Admin GraphQL, webhooks, and session storage included.

**Why merchants and partners choose ApexBuild**
- 🧩 **Installable, not a mockup.** Runs in your admin with `shopify app dev`.
- 🛍️ **Grounded in your store.** Connect your shop and apps are generated against your real products, collections, and scopes.
- ✅ **Built for Shopify by default.** Every build passes a compliance gate and ships mandatory privacy webhooks.
- ⚡ **Minutes, not weeks.** Skip the OAuth/webhook/Polaris plumbing and go straight to the feature.
- 📦 **App Store ready.** Each project includes a pre-filled submission checklist.
- 🔓 **You own the code.** Export the full project anytime.

**Popular things people build**
Low-stock alerts · bulk price editors · order-tagging automations · product
badges · discount campaigns · VIP customer segments · sales dashboards ·
back-in-stock waitlists · fulfillment boards.

**How it works**
1. Describe your app idea.
2. ApexBuild plans it, minimizes scopes, and writes the code.
3. Review the Built-for-Shopify score, download, and run `shopify app dev`.
